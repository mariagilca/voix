## About voix

We are based in Moldova, inside an IT park that brings together young, sharp
minds building the next generation of products and studios.

### Our story

voix grew from the belief that great work should have a clear, confident home
online, without the drag of licenses or heavy tooling.

### Mission

Help you present your work with clarity and credibility, through a portfolio
that feels bespoke and easy to keep current.

### Why choose us

You get a fast, opinionated build, professional writing, and a care plan that
keeps your portfolio updated without extra software fees.
