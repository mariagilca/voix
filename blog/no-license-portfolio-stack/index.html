The no-license portfolio stack

May 29, 2025 · One min read
Voix Studio
Portfolio studio

Voix sites are built on an open-source stack, which means you never pay software license fees to keep your portfolio live.

Here is what that means in practice:

  • No monthly per-seat fees
  • No paywalled publishing tools
  • No surprise charges when your site grows

You pay for the build once, then an annual care plan if you want ongoing updates. Hosting on GitHub Pages is free, and custom domains are billed at cost.

This structure keeps your portfolio lean, predictable, and fully in your control.
