The six beats of a strong case study

August 1, 2025 · One min read
Voix Studio
Portfolio studio

Great case studies are short and structured. We use six beats so every project reads quickly and still feels human.

Quick rule

If a reader cannot summarize the project in 20 seconds, it is too long.

The six beats​

  1. The context
  2. The challenge
  3. Your approach
  4. The craft or tools
  5. The outcome
  6. The takeaway

Keep each beat to 1-3 sentences. Add one metric or quote if you have it.
