Make your copy scannable

Clear writing helps your voice land faster. These patterns keep pages easy to read without losing personality.

Use short sections​

Break long paragraphs into quick blocks:

  • One idea per paragraph
  • A sentence with a metric
  • A sentence that signals your approach

Highlight proof points​
